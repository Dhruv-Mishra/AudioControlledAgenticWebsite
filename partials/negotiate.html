    The playbook

    Three openings to reach for

    Field-tested ways to start a quote without leaving margin on the table.

    • Anchor high

      Open at $2.85/mi.

      Drop to the 7-day average only on the second pass. The first number always sets the ceiling.

    • Bundle the backhaul

      Two for one.

      Offer the carrier the return load at posted rate. Trades a 6-8% discount on the outbound for a guaranteed RPM round-trip.

    • Pay-fast premium

      Net-7 minus 4%.

      Offer a four-percent rate cut for net-7 terms instead of net-30. Most owner-operators take it.
