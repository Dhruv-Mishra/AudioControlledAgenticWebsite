Before you ask

Three things we get asked weekly

  • How do carriers get paid?

    Net-30 by default, net-7 with a 2% quick-pay discount. Wire, ACH, or factor — we don’t care which.

  • Can I track a load mid-transit?

    Yes. Open the map and click any in-transit pin, or ask Jarvis “where is LD-10824?”

  • What if a load is delayed?

    Tap the load row on the dispatch board and click Escalate. A senior dispatcher gets paged within sixty seconds.
